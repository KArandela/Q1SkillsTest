About Our Club

Join us in the exciting world of robotics where we build, program, and compete with robots. Whether you're a beginner or have experience, there's a place for you in our club!

Why Join Us?

The Robotics Club helps you build skills, friendships, and futures. You'll learn:

  • Hands-on STEM skills
  • Creativity & problem-solving
  • Teamwork and leadership
  • Real-world robotics skills

Weekly Schedule

  1. Monday - Planning & Learning (1 hour)
  2. Wednesday - Building & Coding (1-2 hours)
  3. Friday - Testing & Challenges (1-2 hours)
  4. Saturday - Special Events (optional)
